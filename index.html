How it works

Amplitude Modulation (AM) modifies the amplitude of a carrier wave in proportion to a message signal.

sAM(t) = [Ac + Amsin(2πfmt)] × sin(2πfct)

Frequency Modulation (FM) modifies the frequency of the carrier wave in proportion to a message signal.

sFM(t) = Acsin(2πfct - βcos(2πfmt))
