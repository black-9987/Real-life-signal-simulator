How it works

Amplitude Modulation (AM) modifies the amplitude of a high-frequency carrier wave in proportion to a low-frequency message signal.

y(t) = [Ac + Amsin(2πfmt)] × sin(2πfct)

When μ > 1, over-modulation occurs, causing phase reversal and envelope distortion.
